Computer Lab

There are 200 computers in our labs currently. They are state-of-the-art with multimedia, most of them with 18″ LCD monitors that are easier on eyes, generate less heat and consume less power thereby keeping the classroom atmosphere comfortable for students. We use our own LAN with Windows 10 for teaching-learning, documentation and presentations.
